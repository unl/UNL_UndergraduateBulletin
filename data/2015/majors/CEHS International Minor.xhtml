DESCRIPTION

The College of Education and Human Sciences offers a minor in global studies to students in the human sciences. Plan A includes an education abroad experience. Plan B does not have an education abroad experience and must be taken with another 12-credit-hour minor. Contact Dr. Beth Doll at bdoll2@unl.edu or 402-472-2238.

REQUIREMENTS FOR MINOR OFFERED BY DEPARTMENT

Minor–Plan A Requirements – 18 hours

Select 6-12 hours from the following:

CEHS 265 (3 hrs) or CEHS 467/867 (3 hrs) or CEHS 494/894 (3-6 hrs)

Select 6-12 hours from the following:

CYAF 495/895 (3 hrs); EDPS 121 or EDPS 478/878 (3 hrs ea); EDPS 498/898 (1-6 hrs); NUTR 253 or NUTR 205 (3 hrs ea); TEAC 413/813 (1-6 hrs); TEAC 433/833 or TEAC 454B/854B (3 hrs ea); SLPA 488/888 (3 hrs); any ethnic studies course (3 hrs); any applicable courses from the College of Arts and Science Global Studies major.

Minor–Plan B Requirements – 12 hours

This minor must be taken with another 12 credit hour minor.

Select 3-6 hours from the following:

CEHS 265 or CEHS 467/867 (3 hrs ea)

Select 6-9 hours from the following:

CEHS 494/894 (3-6 hrs); CYAF 495/895 (3 hrs); EDPS 121 or EDPS 478/878 (3 hrs ea); EDPS 498/898 (1-6 hrs); NUTR 253 or NUTR 205 (3 hrs ea); TEAC 413/813 (1-6 hrs); TEAC 433/833 or TEAC 454B/854B (3 hrs ea); SLPA 488/888 (3 hrs); any ethnic studies course (3 hrs); any applicable courses from the College of Arts and Science Global Studies major.
