The design/technical production emphasis is for those desiring concentrated training/education in scenic, lighting, sound, and costume design, and technical theatre production. The training the student receives in this program allows him or her to seek an entry-level position in professional theatre, television, or film industries, or pursue further education at a graduate school or internship program. It is the goal of the faculty to give the student the classroom instruction, practical experience, and individual attention needed to form clear goals pertaining to a career in technical theatre.

ADMISSION

Major/Department Admission

Any student who meets college admission requirements may declare a major in theatre–design and technical production. Contact with the Johnny Carson School of Theatre and Film office is required to communicate the intended emphasis prior to admission.

ADDITIONAL MAJOR REQUIREMENTS

Prerequisite Requirements/Advancement in Major

  • Students in the Theatre-Design and Technical Production emphasis are expected to maintain a 2.5 cumulative GPA throughout their career. Transcripts will be reviewed periodically to ensure satisfactory progress. Failure to maintain a 2.5 GPA may result in dismissal from the program.

Grade Rules

  • All courses taken for major requirements must be for letter grade, unless the course is only offered Pass/No Pass.
  • Minimum grade for all major requirements is C.
