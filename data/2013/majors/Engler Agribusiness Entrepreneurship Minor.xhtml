COLLEGE: Agricultural Sciences & Natural Resources

DEGREE OFFERED: Minor only

HOURS REQUIRED: 12 or 18

MINOR AVAILABLE: Yes

DESCRIPTION

Coordinator: Assistant Professor Adam Liska, 246 Chase Hall

Coordinator: Dr. Thomas Field, 212 Miller Hall

Website: http://casnr.unl.edu/engler

The Engler Agribusiness Entrepreneurship Minor offers the knowledge and expertise students will need to create a successful agribusiness venture. It provides skills to evaluate the feasibility of a business idea, identify effective marketing strategies, understand business structure and financing and develop a sound business plan. It provides internship, travel abroad and networking experiences. The minor is for students who want to create and own their own businesses or expand existing businesses by creating new value-added enterprises.

Two minor plans are available:

12-Credit-Hour Plan:

  • Overall 12 credits in approved courses at or above the 300 level including these two required courses:
  • EAEP 488/HORT 488 Business Management for Agricultural Enterprises
  • AGRI 495A Internship in Entrepreneurship
  • In addition, 6 credits from the following electives:
  • AGRI 310 Study Tours in International Agriculture
  • ENTR 321/MGMT 321 Entrepreneurship & Innovation in Organizations
  • ENTR 322 Family Business Management
  • ENTR 421 Entrepreneurship & Venture Management
  • ENTR 422 Small Business Management or ENTR 422A Small Business Owner (but not both)
  • MNGT 423 Small Business Growth & Development
  • AECN 471 Agricultural Marketing & Product Development I
  • AECN 472 Agricultural Marketing & Product Development II
  • University Honors Seminar 395H Entrepreneurship Theory & Practice

18-Credit-Hour Plan:

  • Overall 18 credits in approved courses including the following required courses:
  • EAEP 101 Introductory Seminar on Opportunities in Entrepreneurship
  • EAEP 288/HORT 288 Agribusiness Entrepreneurship or EAEP 121/ENTR 121 Introduction to Entrepreneurship Management (but not both)
  • EAEP 275/AECN 275 Agribusiness Entrepreneurial Finance
  • EAEP 488/HORT 488 Business Management for Agricultural Enterprises
  • AGRI 495A Internship in Entrepreneurship

  • AECN 225/MRKT 225 Agribusiness Entrepreneurship in Food Products Marketing

  • AGRI 310 Study Tours in International Agriculture

  • ENTR 422 Small Business Management or ENTR 422A Small Business Owner (but not both)
  • MNGT 423 Small Business Growth & Development
  • AECN 471 Agricultural Marketing & Product Development I
  • AECN 472 Agricultural Marketing & Product Development II
