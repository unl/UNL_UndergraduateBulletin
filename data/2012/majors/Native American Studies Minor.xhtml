Native American Studies Minor

COLLEGE: Arts & Sciences

MAJOR: Native American Studies (Minor only)

DEGREES OFFERED: Minor only

HOURS REQUIRED: 18

MINOR AVAILABLE: Yes

CHIEF ADVISER: Thomas Gannon

DESCRIPTION

Faculty: Hames (anthropology and geography); Awakuni-Swetland, McCollough (anthropology and geography/ethnic studies); Gannon, Kaye (English); Wishart (geography); Akers, Jacobs, Smith, Wunder (history); Snowden (law); Moore, Whitbeck (sociology), Willis-Esqueda (psychology/ethnic studies)

Native American Studies offers a variety of courses and includes a minor in Native American Studies.
