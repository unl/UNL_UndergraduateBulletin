The microbiology is an interdepartmental major that offers educational opportunities in various areas of microbiology leading to a bachelor of science degree in microbiology. The training offered is suitable for a professional career in microbiology, which may lead to employment in the food industry, clinical microbiology, biotechnology, and pharmaceuticals; or federal agencies such as the Food and Drug Administration, U.S. Department of Agriculture, U.S. Public Health Service, and Environmental Protection Agency. The program is also suitable as preparation for graduate studies leading to academic careers and professional careers in medicine, dentistry, veterinary medicine, pharmacy, and health-related fields.

Students interested in majoring in microbiology are advised to make an appointment with the academic adviser.

Students concerned about their preparation for college-level biology should take BIOS 101 and BIOS 101L prior to BIOS 102. Please consult your adviser if in doubt.
